# Qr-code-checkout-sysytem
<h1>This is an early preview</h1>


<p> the qrcode system was made to stop the hustle and bustle of waiters moving from table to table to get orders and send it to the kitchen rather the user can scan the qr code on their table order from the client side webapp whilst in real time the orders can or are received by the kitchendashboard and after the prepration is done the customers are then notified on their side that the orders are completed all of this happening in real time tis is to also ease the pain of tracking normal paper orders in resturants</p>
